Dr. Zhang's Questline

This document outlines the events surrounding Dr. Zhang's quests.  The base event is the players meeting Dr. Zhang and being
given the ultimatium to help find the ingredients to perfect his potion for immortality, or leave him to do his research
in peace.  

EVENT Zhang Ignore - Spawn rate of zombies increases every day for 16? days.  After 24 in-game days, the world becomes much 
darker (or some sort of status is applied to the player) and a notice of a feeling that great evil is lose.  At this point
Zhang comes out every night and attempts to find and kill the player.
